How It Works

We Source the Flowers

Our flowers are sourced from special flower growers in South America. They're harvested in an eco-friendly way, and immediately sent to the US where our expert floral designers create beautiful bouquets for our customers.

You Choose Your Flower Frequency

We offer two flower packages to choose from: weekly or monthly.

Weekly Flowers

  • Receive fresh flowers delivered each Monday
  • Flowers are always in season and fresh
  • Guaranteed to last until your next arrangement arrives

Monthly Flowers

  • Receive fresh flowers delivered the first Monday of the month
  • Flowers are always in season and fresh
  • Guaranteed to brighten someone's day

Enjoy Your Flowers

Once you've decided on your flower frequency, you can expect your first flower arrangement the following Monday. Enjoy!
